## üõ†Ô∏è Core Systems & Mechanics

### 1. Signal Attention Engine (SAE)
The SAE tracks how users interact with content across four modalities:
- **Glances:** Brief visibility as you drift past a post.
- **Holds:** Sustained attention (3+ seconds) indicating intent.
- **Returns:** Re-visiting a specific signal after a period of absence.
- **Deep Interactions:** Active resonance (Reactions, Comments, Captures).

### 2. Intensity Calibration (Dynamic Feed)
Users control their "Aperture Intensity" (0-100%):
- **Low Intensity (0-30%):** Prioritizes mutual signals, calm energy levels, and established connections.
- **Mid Intensity (30-70%):** A balance of the inner circle and interesting "drift" from the collective.
- **High Intensity (70-100%):** Prioritizes novelty, global resonance, and diverse frequencies.

### 3. Anti-Viral Safeguards
To prevent "shallow noise," the ranking system detects **Spike Fatigue**:
- Posts with high Glance counts but extremely low Hold Ratios are penalized.
- Sustained Attention (High Hold-to-Glance ratio) is rewarded with "Synchronicity" status.

### 4. Gemini AI Integration
- **Identity Manifestation:** Gemini generates unique personas (names, handles, bios, brand colors) based on a user's "vibe" input.
- **Aperture Insights:** In the Resonance Lab, Gemini analyzes private interaction data to provide poetic, actionable insights about a creator's presence.
- **Transparency Hints:** AI-generated explanations of why specific signals are appearing in a user's feed.

## üöÄ Working Processes
1. **Emit Signal:** Every interaction (View, React, Comment) emits a "Signal" that updates the mutual resonance score between two users.
2. **Decay Logic:** Signals lose 5% strength per day of inactivity to ensure the "Inner Circle" reflects current life phases.
3. **Capture:** Sharing a post creates a "Memory Artifact" in your own Sanctuary, preserving the drift.
4. **Anchor Identity:** Guests can "Anchor" at any time, converting their ephemeral session into a permanent sanctuary using Gemini-assisted creation.
